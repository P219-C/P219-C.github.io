Hello! I am Pablo, a geophysicist

In February 2021, after two years of hard work, I graduated with distinction.

Since 2016, I've been involved in various mining and energy exploration projects, both as a student and professional, in Mexico and Australia. Some of these include activities in a gold mine in the Mexican desert, looking for production opportunities in mature oilfields in Mexico, and technical analysis of onshore and offshore reservoirs in Australia for hydrocarbon and geothermal purposes.
